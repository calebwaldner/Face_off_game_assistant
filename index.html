This was created for classroom use. This app acts as a sort of ‘spinning category selector’, so ideally the screen is displayed for all contestants to see. First, provide a number for ‘% of drawing secondary question’, then click 'Update'. Change any of the categories you’d like. There are two types of categories, primary and secondary. Out of the primary and secondary questions, you can add subcategories which each have a chance to be landed on equally. The game is played by holding the space button on the keyboard to 'spin' or cycle randomly through question categories. Close this menu by pressing the plus symbol again and begin playing!
